Chuck Newman

Real Estate Specialist | Reliance Realty Chuck Newman has been a key part of Reliance Realty since 2012, known for delivering strong results across New York City's complex rental market. With over a decade of experience, he specializes in maximizing rental income for landlords through unit conversions, value-add renovations, and innovative leasing strategies. Chuck began his career managing a 300-unit doorman building in Turtle Bay, where he led successful unit reconfigurations that dramatically boosted rents and reduced vacancies. He later scaled this strategy across rent-stabilized portfolios in Upper Manhattan, the Bronx, and Queens—consistently increasing NOI and outperforming projections for major landlords and developers. As a recognized expert in housing voucher programs, Chuck has helped landlords navigate policy shifts and pandemic-era challenges by leveraging programs like CityFHEPS and Section 8. His relationships with caseworkers, housing officials, and city agencies—including direct access to the NYC Mayor’s Office—enable faster approvals and higher returns for owners. Expertise Includes:
  •  Housing Voucher Strategy: End-to-end support for Section 8 and CityFHEPS placements
  • Portfolio Repositioning: Unit conversions, rent roll improvement, and vacancy reduction
  • Value-Add Renovations: From single units to large-scale, multi-unit renovations
  • Strategic Asset Planning: Ideal for owners refinancing, selling, or stabilizing properties
From high-end buildings in Midtown to workforce housing near major institutions like Columbia and Mount Sinai, Chuck brings the insight, network, and execution needed to unlock hidden value—regardless of market conditions.
